What is meal prep

"Meal prepping is a valuable practice for individuals looking to maintain a healthy and organized diet. For beginners, meal prepping involves planning and preparing meals in advance to support their fitness goals and dietary needs. It typically includes simple recipes with basic ingredients, ensuring a balanced intake of proteins, carbohydrates, and fats. This approach helps beginners establish a routine and develop healthy eating habits. On the other hand, intermediate meal prepping is suitable for those with a deeper understanding of nutrition and a desire to explore more diverse and flavorful meals. Intermediate meal preps often involve experimenting with different recipes, incorporating a wider variety of ingredients, and focusing on specific macronutrient ratios tailored to individual preferences or fitness objectives. These meal preps may also involve advanced techniques such as portion control and advanced cooking methods. Regardless of the level, meal prepping offers convenience, saves time, and promotes a consistent and nutritious diet."
